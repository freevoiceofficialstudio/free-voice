About Free Voice

Free Voice is a modern AI-powered voice changing platform designed to help users transform their voice in real time for games, calls, voice chats and creative projects.

Our system allows users to record, upload and convert their voice using realistic AI-based voice profiles. Downloaded voices work offline, while live voice changing is securely protected behind a premium membership.

Free Voice does not store user voice recordings on any server. All voice processing happens locally on the user’s device to ensure privacy and security.

This product is developed and maintained by Subhan Ahmad. We aim to provide a simple, fast and ethical voice transformation tool suitable for both casual and professional use.
