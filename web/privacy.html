Privacy Policy

At Free Voice, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

We collect basic account information such as your name and email address through Google Sign-In. This data is used solely for authentication and account management.

Voice Data

Free Voice does not store user voice recordings or processed audio on any server. All voice processing happens locally on your device.

Payments

Payments are processed securely via Stripe Checkout. Free Voice does not store or process your payment details. Stripe may collect information as described in their own privacy policy.

Membership & Security

Membership status and expiry information are stored securely to enforce feature access in real time. Attempts to bypass membership restrictions are blocked automatically.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for how they use AI-generated or modified voices. Free Voice must not be used for impersonation, fraud or unlawful activities.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of Free Voice indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
